A COAT by WILLIAM BUTLER YATES


I made my song a coat
Covered with
  • embroideries
Out of
  • old
    • mythologies
      From heel to throat;
      But the
    • fools caught it,
      Wore it in the world’s eyes
      As though
      1. they’d wrought it.
      2. Song, let them take it
        For there’s more enterprise
          In walking
          • naked.
